### Issue #9 - Low Flexibility Checklist

- [ ] **[Accessibility](https://example.com/accessibility)** 
  Ensuring the application is usable for people of all abilities and disabilities.

- [ ] **[Browser Compatibility](https://example.com/browser-compatibility)** 
  The application should provide a consistent experience across different web browsers.

- [ ] **[Form Validation](https://example.com/form-validation)** 
  Implement proper form validation to enhance user experience and data integrity.

- [ ] **[Responsive Design](https://example.com/responsive-design)** 
  The application must be usable across a wide range of devices and window or screen sizes.

- [ ] **[Performance Optimization](https://example.com/performance-optimization)** 
  Reduce the load time and optimize the application performance.

- [ ] **[SEO Best Practices](https://example.com/seo-best-practices)** 
  Optimize your site to rank better in search engine results.

- [ ] **[Content Delivery Network (CDN)](https://example.com/cdn)** 
  Use CDNs to host static resources for better load performance.
